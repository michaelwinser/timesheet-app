Mock 4: Invoice Status Transitions

Draft ‚Üí Sent ‚Üí Paid

DRAFT STATUS

Draft invoices can be edited You can regenerate, add/remove entries, or delete this invoice.

SENT STATUS

Invoice sent to client Time entries are locked and cannot be changed. Invoice can still be exported.

PAID STATUS

Invoice paid Archived for record-keeping. Can be viewed and exported but not modified.
Time entry locking: When invoice status changes to "Sent", all included time entries become immutable (invoiced state). Reverting to Draft does not unlock them.
